Popular Turkish actress

1. Hande Ercel

Hande Ercel is arguably the most well-known and beautiful actress from Turkey. She is known for starring in TV series such as Güneşin Kızları, Siyah İnci, and most famously as Hayat Uzun in Aşk Laftan Anlamaz, a role for which she received widespread fame across the Middle East and landed her several awards, including two Golden Butterfly Awards.

In 2020, Ercel landed the leading role in the popular TV series Sen Çal Kapımı; this romantic comedy was broadcast to over 100 countries.
